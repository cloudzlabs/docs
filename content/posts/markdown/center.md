## Shortcode: center

Center align you content.

### center: Parameters

- Markdown content between opening and closing tags.

### center: Usage Example
```golang
{{%/* center */%}}
_Center Aligned Text_
{{%/* /center */%}}
```

**Output**

{{% center %}}
_Center Aligned Text_
{{% /center %}}
